23 - Merge Calculator & Guided Quote Entry

What & Why
Replace the current split homepage layout with a single unified "Get Started" section. Customers should choose between two equal-weight paths:
- Instant Pricing
- Guided Questions

This removes confusion from having multiple competing entry points and gives visitors one clear place to begin.

Done looks like
- The homepage has one unified quote/booking section with a tab switch:
  - Instant Pricing
  - Guided Questions
- Both tabs have equal visual weight and short descriptor copy:
  - Instant Pricing: "Build your move and see pricing now."
  - Guided Questions: "Not sure what you need? We’ll walk you through it."
- Switching tabs renders the calculator or the embedded chatbot beneath the switch
- The BookingChatbot supports embedded mode:
  - no dialog-only chrome
  - no close button
  - renders cleanly inline
- CTA buttons and service shortcut links scroll to this unified section
- Residential / calculator-friendly shortcuts preselect Instant Pricing
- Less pricing-friendly services can default to Guided Questions
- The floating chatbot launcher is removed from the homepage so there is no duplicate guided entry point
- The manual fallback quote form remains lower on the page for custom/non-calculator jobs
- Existing pricing logic and backend submission behavior stay unchanged
- Mobile layout stays clean and usable

Out of scope
- Changes to pricing logic
- Changes to quote calculations
- Backend API changes
- Adding new guided-chatbot question steps
- Redesigning the calculator itself
