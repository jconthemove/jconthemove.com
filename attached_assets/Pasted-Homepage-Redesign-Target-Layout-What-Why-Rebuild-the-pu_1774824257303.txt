Homepage Redesign — Target Layout
What & Why
Rebuild the public homepage (home.tsx) to match the target design screenshots provided by the user. The current page is a basic video + grid layout. The target is a polished, conversion-focused moving company landing page with a hero split layout, quick-book panel, live pricing calculator, trust signals, recent jobs gallery, and a full inline quote form.

Trust badge row beneath the hero: Licensed & Insured, 5-Star Local Reputation, Fast Response
"Why People Choose JC ON THE MOVE" section with stat blocks (500+ Jobs, 5.0 Rating, Same-Day, Ironwood) and three checkmark trust lines
"Recent Jobs" photo grid using existing attached_assets job photos, each card showing crew count, hours, and price

Testimonials section with 3 short customer quotes and star ratings
Full inline "Get Your Free Quote" form (service type icon selector, first/last name, email, phone, origin/destination address, preferred date, property size, additional details, promo code, photo upload, submit CTA)
